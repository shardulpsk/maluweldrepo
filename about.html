About Us
Set up in 1992 by Mr Venugopal Malu and his brother, Mr Shrawan Kumar Malu, Nagpur-based MEPL manufactures a variety of welding electrodes, including mild-steel general-purpose electrodes, hard-facing electrodes, and stainless steel and cutting electrodes. The production facilities are in Nagpur and Haridwar; a facility has been recently set up in Bhubaneshwar.We are working in this profession with good amount of workforce and experts. We have 10 years of experience and delivering quality of services and satisfaction to our customers. Maluweld is ISO 9001 certified company. Our quality management is best in a market. Our products are distributed across all parts of India and also in various countries across the world. Our team members are experience with wide arange of experience in welding field.
